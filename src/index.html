Program Descriptions – Computer Science  & Computer Information Systems Degrees

Bachelor of Science – Computer Science

The BSCS at Averett teaches fundamental programming, web development, and simulation skills, underpinned by statistics and calculus courses designed to teach quantitative reasoning. Data structures, architectures, and machine language are also covered.

Bachelor of Science – Computer Information Systems

Students completing the Bachelor of Science program in CIS are positioned to make a valuable impact on the information-based and data-rich world of business and technology. CIS majors study the disciplines of business and information technology to learn how to analyze, design, develop, implement and manage computer-based solutions allowing organizations to improve, remain competitive, and obtain a sustainable competitive advantage in the globalized world.   The CIS curriculum is grounded in the fundamentals of business, decision making, current and emerging computing technologies.
